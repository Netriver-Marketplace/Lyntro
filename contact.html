Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Register" button in the header, fill in your details, and you'll be ready to start buying and selling in minutes!

Is it free to use NaijaMarket?

Yes! Creating an account and browsing products is completely free. We only charge a small commission on successful sales.

How do I pay for items?

We support multiple payment methods including bank transfer, Paystack, and cash on delivery for eligible items.

Is my information safe?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption, password hashing, and other security measures to protect your data.

How do I report a problem?

Use the contact form above and select "Report a Problem" as the subject. Our team will investigate promptly.
